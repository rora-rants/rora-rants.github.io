Why This Website?

This website's reason for existing is to provide a digital space where I can rant and muse about stuff. As evidenced by the name, the main purpose is ranting about stuff (mostly tech) but I might add some musings about things I find interesting or noteworthy.

Now you may ask... if it's just a place to record your musings:

  • Why not just write in your diary? Well, I enjoy writing static webpages with HTML, CSS and maybe some JavaScript here & there.
  • Why not use some blogging software and host it there? See above :) The point is to let me write stuff quickly and if I feel like adding styling or other stuff, I can.
  • Why is it a publicly accessible website? So that I can easily access it myself from different devices, work on it across different devices and share it with people I know.

If you stumble upon this website and you have no idea who I am, then hi. If you're a web scraper, hello to you too I guess but I don't you'll find much that's particularly interesting to feed into your LLM or however you intend to use the data you scraped.

Inspired by ToastyTech, SoraNews24 and my personal predisposition towards ranting... it is indeed a personal hobby of mine even though not everyone likes it!
